Required Documents for Verification

  • Original Student ID Card - Must be presented in person

Recovery Process Steps

1

Visit Hall Committee Office

Go to the Hall Committee Office during office hours with your required documents.

2

Identity Verification

Show your documents to the hall authority for identity verification.

3

Request Password Reset

Inform the authority that you need to reset your meal system password.

4

Receive New Password

The hall authority will reset your password and provide you with a new temporary password.

5

Change Password After Login

Login with the temporary password and change it to your preferred password immediately.
